WORLD IS YOUR OYSTER

Maybe I'm just a small traveler compare to the other people on this planet, but my story just began. There are so many beautiful places on this planet, which I've never visited but one day I will.

My story began over 5 years ago, when I decided to leave my home country (Czech Republic) and see the rest of the world. At that moment I thought that I will be gone just for a year, but that one trip changed my life.

For a first time in my life, I took a plane flight to London just by myself. I was alone in a strange country with different language and culture. But the day when I saw London for a first time, was the day when a real traveler inside of me was awaken. Since that time in 2012, I've visited around 10 different countries. I've visited big cities, but also smaller ones. So let's take a plane flight to some of my favorite destinations...
